About Me

I'm a 22 year old Applied Software Engineering Major at NKU with a minor in Computer Science. I adore the process of software development the more I learn about it, and have resolved myself to being a full-stack application developer. I live near Lawrenceburg, Indiana and currently work for a grocery store. I've only recently begun to commit to the software engineering career as I finally feel I've developed enough skill.

I love games of all types, from board to card to video, games have always been a hobby of mine. I may not be the best at them but that's not what's important here. I love playing with our family dog Sugar. To give you an idea of what she's like, for christmas I bought one of those rubber chickens as a gag gift and she now treats it as though it is her child.

I'm a tenacious, hopelessly optimistic procrastinator fueled by spite and a love for those around me. I'll never give up until the last possible moment, and put my all into every single thing I do. In other words, I love my friends, I hate my friends, and they're my reason for pushing myself as hard as I do, even if I'm not the best at scheduling.
